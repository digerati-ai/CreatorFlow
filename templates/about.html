About CreatorFlow

CreatorFlow is a content publishing platform built for TikTok creators who want a simple, transparent, and compliant way to publish their original video content.

What We Do

CreatorFlow connects to your TikTok account through TikTok's official authorization system and provides a clean, intuitive interface for publishing your videos. You get full control over your content's privacy settings, interaction preferences, and commercial disclosures — all in one place before you hit publish.

How It Works

1

Connect

Securely authorize CreatorFlow with your TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth flow.

2

Upload & Configure

Upload your video, set your title, choose privacy settings, and configure interaction preferences.

3

Preview & Publish

Review everything, consent to TikTok's terms, and publish directly or send to your TikTok drafts.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparency, user control, and platform compliance. CreatorFlow is built on TikTok's official Content Posting API and adheres to TikTok's developer guidelines, content sharing guidelines, and UX requirements. We do not add watermarks to your content, we do not access data beyond what's necessary, and we give you full control over every aspect of your posts.

Contact Us
